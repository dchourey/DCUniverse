Research Work

Currently, I am working on cosmology using a phenomenon called Weak Lensing to derive fundamental properties of our Universe like matter density, dark matter distribution, etc. Weak Lensing or Cosmic shear is a phenomenon of bending of light because of an in-between matter distribution such as a galaxy cluster. This causes distortions in the shape of background elliptical galaxies. By studying such distortions, we can infer several important aspects of our late-time Universe. Here are some of my past research projects :

Star Formation at Bright Rimmed Clouds

Under the supervision of Dr. Jessy Jose (IISER, Tirupati) and Dr. Anandmayee Tej (IIST, Trivandrum), this project investigates low mass star formation processes at two bright rimmed clouds named BRC13 and BRC14. Using multi-wavelength analysis, I estimated the star formation rate and star formation efficiency of BRCs to get a statistical notion of star formation processes via radiation-driven implosion.

  • Thesis

Frequency Forecasting using Artificial Neural Networks

This project deals with the accurate prediction of the power grid frequency. Supervised by Dr. Anup Mishra (BIT, Durg), it utilizes a soft computing technique called Neural Networks. The frequency of a power grid primarily depends on the user-side load. Random switching of load causes grid frequency to fluctuate. This fluctuation is very dangerous beyond a certain limit and may cause the entire grid to collapse.

  • Thesis

Role of System Inertia in Frequency Forecast

In the second phase of frequency forecast franchise, this project proposes an extreamely useful technique to account for system inertia in the process of frequency forecast. This technique involves another soft computing method called fuzzy logic. Combining fuzzy logic and neural network, I showed that the accuracy of the forecast can be increased up to 98%.

  • NASA/ADS

Frequency Spectrum based Primary Grid Frequency Control

Once the grid frequency forecast is available to the dispatch center, the next step involves controlling the frequency within the standard range. In this project, I have suggested a novel control system design based on the Fourier spectrum of forecasted frequency.
